## 1

$$
U_{\mathrm{eff}}(r)
=\frac{l^2}{2mr^2}+\frac{1}{r^3}
$$

$$
\frac{\mathrm{d}U_{\mathrm{eff}}(r)}{\mathrm{d}r}
=-\frac{l^2}{mr^3}-\frac{3}{r^4}
<0
$$

$$
\lim_{r\to 0^+}U_{\mathrm{eff}}(r)
=+\infty
$$

$$
\lim_{r\to +\infty}U_{\mathrm{eff}}(r)
=0
$$
