## 2.1 洛伦兹不变性

### 2.1.1 旋转

2维旋转

$$
x\to x'=x\cos\theta+y\sin\theta
$$

$$
y\to y'=-x\sin\theta+y\cos\theta
$$

$$
\begin{bmatrix}
x \\
y
\end{bmatrix} \to 
\begin{bmatrix}
x' \\
y'
\end{bmatrix}
=\begin{bmatrix}
\cos\theta &\sin\theta \\
-\sin\theta &\cos\theta
\end{bmatrix}
\begin{bmatrix}
x \\
y
\end{bmatrix}
$$

$$
x_i\to x_i'=R_{ij}x_j,\quad 
x_i=\begin{bmatrix}
x \\
y
\end{bmatrix},\quad i=1,2
$$

行矢量：

$$
x^i
=\begin{bmatrix}
x &y
\end{bmatrix}
\to \begin{bmatrix}
x' &y'
\end{bmatrix}
=\begin{bmatrix}
x &y
\end{bmatrix}
\begin{bmatrix}
\cos\theta &-\sin\theta \\
\sin\theta &\cos\theta
\end{bmatrix}
=x^j \left(R^{\mathrm{T}} \right)^{ji}
$$

$$
R^{\mathrm{T}} R = I
$$

$$
\left(R^{\mathrm{T}} \right)_{ij}R_{ik}=\delta_{ik}
$$

$$
x^i x_i
=x^2+y^2
$$

$$
x'^ix'_i
=x^j\left(R^{\mathrm{T}} \right)^{ji} R_{ik}x_k
=x^j\delta_{jk}x_k
=x^jx_j
$$
